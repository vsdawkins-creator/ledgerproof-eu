**Document version:** v1.0 (May 28, 2026)

**Fixed external constraint:** EU AI Act Article 50 enforces August 2, 2026

---

## Operating thesis

LedgerProof is the open-protocol implementation reference for EU AI Act Article 50. The seed-to-Series-A window is six months. In that window we must convert a regulatory deadline into a category, and a category into a defensible market position. The plan does not depend on enforcement actions happening; it depends on regulators, Big-4 firms, and enterprise General Counsels having a clear, defensible answer when they are asked "how do you prove this?"

The plan is structured as **seven tracks** executing in parallel against **five milestone gates**.

## Cadence

### Weekly
- Monday 09:00 CET: founder sync with eng + spec + ops leads (15 min, async-preferred)
- Tuesday 09:00 CET: outbound send window opens; no outbound on Monday or Friday
- Thursday 16:00 CET: pipeline review (sales + partnerships)
- Friday 12:00 CET: regulator-and-standards review

### Bi-weekly
- Investor update (2 paragraphs + dashboard link) to seed investors + select Series A leads
- Foundation board prep (when operating)

### Monthly
- Audit-Ready Compliance Stamp PDFs issued to all paying customers
- Public Foundation transparency report

### Quarterly
- Roadmap publication on `spec.ledgerproofhq.io/roadmap`
- Pricing review

## Risk register

| Risk | Likelihood | Impact | Mitigation |
|---|---|---|---|
| Article 50 enforcement delayed beyond Aug 2 | Low | High | Narrative shifts to ISO 42001 + DORA; pilots already running on existing standards |
| Code of Practice arrives with mandates that break LPR v1.1 schema | Medium | Medium | Profile system absorbs; LPR v1.2 already scoped to add adaptation hooks |
| Closed-protocol competitor with VC distribution outpaces seed cycle | Medium | Medium | Open protocol + Foundation + IETF positioning make the moat structural, not feature-based |
| Big-4 firms decline partnership terms | Medium | Medium | Value-for-value framing tested with all five firms simultaneously; symmetry is leverage |
| Operator outage during high-profile customer demo | Medium | High | Multi-region + Customer-side SDK fallback queue; SLO refund triggers in pilot SOW |
| Founder bandwidth becomes single point of failure | High | High | Hiring sequence above front-loads ops + sales relief; CFO advisor by Day 30 |
| Series A market closes before Day 180 | Medium | Medium | Seed runway carries ~16 months at Day-180 burn; bridge available from existing investors |
