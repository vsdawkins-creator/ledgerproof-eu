## Why no payment?

The Big-4 firms ARE the buyers — through their clients. A paid referral arrangement would (a) trigger their independence policies and disqualify the partnership at procurement review, and (b) signal to their partner committees that LedgerProof is buying placement rather than earning it. The value-for-value framing is what gets these partnerships approved.

The downside is that the firms can recommend competing products simultaneously. That is fine. Article 50 is a moving target, and the consulting firms benefit from a portfolio of recommendations. LedgerProof's job is to be the strongest single recommendation in the open-protocol category — and to be the only one that ships before August 2.
