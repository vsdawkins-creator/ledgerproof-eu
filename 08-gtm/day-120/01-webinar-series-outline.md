# Joint Webinar Series Outline — Big-4 + LedgerProof

**Purpose:** Convert signed Big-4 LOIs into ongoing pipeline through a monthly co-branded webinar series. Each webinar produces three deliverables: a recorded session, a derived whitepaper, and a list of qualified attendee leads filtered by the Big-4 firm's account-team.

**Launch:** Month 1 post-Big-4-LOI signature (expected Day 120 of master plan)
**Cadence:** Monthly, second Tuesday of the month, 15:00 CET (60 min)
**Audience target:** 200+ registered, 80+ live, per session
**Lead handoff:** Big-4 firm's account team owns lead qualification; LedgerProof receives qualified leads with the firm's annotations

---

## Format

| Time | Segment | Lead |
|---|---|---|
| 0–5 min | Welcome + housekeeping | Big-4 host |
| 5–15 min | Regulatory landscape briefing | Big-4 partner |
| 15–35 min | Technical implementation walkthrough | LedgerProof founder/spec lead |
| 35–50 min | Customer panel: one Big-4 client + one LedgerProof reference customer | Big-4 host |
| 50–60 min | Live Q&A (questions pre-screened by Big-4 firm) | Joint |

The 80/20 rule on speaking time: Big-4 leads regulatory interpretation (the firm's brand authority); LedgerProof leads implementation (our brand authority). Neither side overlaps into the other's territory.

**Critical constraint:** The Big-4 firm controls which of its clients are invited. LedgerProof never directly emails the Big-4's named accounts about a co-branded webinar. This preserves the Big-4 firm's client trust and is non-negotiable in the LOI.

## Lead handoff

After each webinar:

1. Big-4 firm's marketing operations receives the full attendee list
2. Big-4 firm's account teams review attendees from their assigned accounts
3. Big-4 firm flags qualified leads with one of three annotations: **firm-engaged** (already a Big-4 client; firm leads follow-up with LedgerProof in observer role), **opportunity** (not a current Big-4 client; firm declines lead; LedgerProof free to pursue), or **decline** (firm requests no LedgerProof outreach)
4. LedgerProof BD lead receives the annotated list within 5 business days of webinar
5. LedgerProof commits to following the firm's annotations exactly; one violation ends the partnership

## Content reuse

Each webinar produces:

1. **Recording** — published to ledgerproofhq.io/webinars and the Big-4 firm's content hub, co-branded
2. **Whitepaper** — 4–6 pages, co-authored, published at Day +30 of webinar
3. **One-page summary** — for sales follow-up
4. **3–5 social-media-ready quotes** from the panel customers
5. **Audit-Ready Compliance Stamp PDF anchoring the webinar transcript** — Foundation publishes; demonstrates the protocol on its own content

## Per-firm replication

The above plan is built for one Big-4 firm. With two firms signed by Day 180, the series runs in parallel — one firm-anchored series each, alternating Tuesdays. Cross-promotion between firms is permitted only if both firms approve.

By Day 365 (one year post-launch), the goal is three Big-4 firms running parallel series. At three firms, total webinar pipeline contribution should be the largest single source of qualified enterprise pipeline.
