## Long description

LedgerProof generates regulator-verifiable receipts for AI-generated outputs from Google Cloud's Vertex AI, Gemini API, and any other AI workload deployed on GCP. Each receipt satisfies EU AI Act Article 50 transparency obligations in a form a regulator can verify in under 10 seconds without trusting the customer or any vendor.

The protocol is published as an IETF Internet-Draft (`draft-dawkins-scitt-ai-article50-00`) and governed by the LedgerProof Foundation. Receipts are signed Ed25519, anchored to Bitcoin mainnet, and verifiable by any third party. Foundation membership is open; multiple operators may issue receipts under the same protocol.

**Native Google Cloud integrations:**
- **Vertex AI:** SDK wraps Vertex AI Prediction, Generative AI Studio, and Model Garden calls; receipts capture model deployment, prompt hash, and output hash.
- **Gemini API:** Pre-built receipt issuance for Gemini 1.5 Pro, Gemini 1.5 Flash, and successors.
- **Google Cloud Logging:** Receipt issuance events stream to Cloud Logging; alerts via Cloud Monitoring.
- **Document AI:** Receipts cover AI-extracted document processing outputs subject to Article 50(4).
- **Workspace integrations:** Gemini in Workspace outputs (Docs, Sheets, Slides, Gmail) can be receipted via the Workspace Add-on.
- **GCP Sovereign Cloud (S3NS, T-Systems):** Receipt operator deployable to Sovereign Cloud regions for EU member states requiring sovereignty controls.

**Coverage:**
- EU AI Act Article 50(1), 50(2), 50(4) — full
- ISO/IEC 42001:2023 — 24 of 38 Annex A controls (all 8 in A.6 AI System Life Cycle)
- NIST AI RMF — Govern, Map, Measure, Manage
- DORA Article 28 — ICT third-party risk evidence
- MiFID II Article 16 — record-keeping
- GDPR Article 17 — soft-delete semantics with schema-level PII rejection
