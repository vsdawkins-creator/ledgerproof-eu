Dear Moody's Innovation Team,

Moody's Analytics is deploying AI across credit analysis, ratings research, and compliance documentation. The value of that deployment depends entirely on the defensibility of the outputs -- in regulatory examination, in litigation, and in client trust.

LedgerProof provides the cryptographic audit trail that makes AI-generated outputs defensible.

We anchor the SHA-256 hash and Ed25519 signature of any document to Bitcoin mainnet via OP_RETURN at the moment of creation. The result is an LPR v1.0 receipt -- a tamper-evident, permanently verifiable provenance record that establishes exactly what was produced and when. It exists on Bitcoin mainnet permanently, independently verifiable by any party, with no reliance on LedgerProof's continued operation. Live on mainnet since May 18, 2026: https://verify.ledgerproofhq.io/r/founding-declaration

Moody's European operations -- ratings research, risk analytics, compliance reporting -- fall within the enforcement scope. When an AI-assisted Moody's analysis is challenged in a regulatory examination or cited in litigation, the LPR receipt is the unalterable ground truth: the document hash, the timestamp, the signature, anchored to a public blockchain no party controls.

The integration is non-invasive. LedgerProof operates as a receipt-generation layer at the point of output: the document hash is passed to our API, anchored to mainnet, and a verifiable receipt URI is returned. No changes to Moody's core analytics infrastructure. No custody, no token, no speculative exposure. Per-receipt pricing with enterprise volume tiers.

Beyond regulatory compliance, there is a client trust dimension: Moody's clients who receive AI-assisted research with an attached LPR receipt have independent, cryptographic confirmation of provenance -- a differentiator as AI output authenticity is increasingly scrutinized.
