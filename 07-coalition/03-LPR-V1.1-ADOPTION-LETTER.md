## Full version (briefing for signature decision)

### What we are asking you to sign

A short public statement (above) endorsing LedgerProof Receipt (LPR) v1.1 as a viable open standard for EU AI Act Article 50 transparency receipts, and committing to evaluate it for adoption over the next 90 days.

You are **not** committing to:
- Pay for LedgerProof
- Use it exclusively
- Abandon C2PA, watermarking, or any other compliance technology you already use
- Change any vendor relationship

You **are** committing to:
- Be named publicly as a "founding adopter" of LPR v1.1 on July 6, 2026
- Actually evaluate it within 90 days (a paper exercise, not a deployment)
- Allow your organization name and logo to appear in launch materials

### Why this matters for your organization

EU AI Act Article 50 enforcement begins August 2, 2026. Penalties for non-compliance reach €15M or 3% of global turnover. The Code of Practice that defines compliance is being finalized in early June 2026. No open protocol covers all four Article 50 sub-obligations except LPR v1.1.

Organizations that adopt early benefit from:

1. **Regulatory recognition** as forward-leaning on AI transparency
2. **Influence over the standard** — early adopters shape v1.2 and v2.0
3. **Implementation lead time** before competitors are forced to adopt under enforcement pressure
4. **Public association** with an EU AI Office-aligned compliance approach

### Why this matters for LedgerProof

A list of named founding adopters transforms LedgerProof from "a startup" into "a coalition." It directly affects:

- The IETF working group's reception of the draft
- The EU AI Office's evaluation of the signatory application
- The C2PA CAWG's willingness to register the assertion namespace
- Every customer conversation from July 6 forward

A single signature from a TVP portfolio company, a major news organization, a regulated EU enterprise, or a large legal/compliance firm is worth weeks of standalone marketing.

### What founding adopters get

1. **Founding Adopter** designation in all LedgerProof launch materials
2. **Charter pricing**: $499/month pilot tier (vs. $1,500 standard), locked for 24 months
3. **Reserved seat** on the LPR Technical Advisory Council (advisory, not fiduciary)
4. **Priority IETF working group representation** through joint submissions
5. **First access** to v1.2 features (audio watermark interoperability, video provenance, etc.)
6. **Joint press coverage** in LedgerProof's July 6 launch press release
