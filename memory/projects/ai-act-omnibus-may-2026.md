## Strategic implications for LedgerProof

### Two campaign peaks instead of one
- **Peak 1 — Aug 2, 2026:** Article 50 enforces. (Original keystone.)
- **Peak 2 — Dec 2, 2026:** Grandfather period ends + nudifier/CSAM ban activates + Omnibus formally adopted (July) settles into enforcement reality.

Campaign budget should be spread across both peaks, not front-loaded to Aug 2.

### Article 5 nudifier ban creates a NEW buyer segment
Consumer AI image-generation vendors (Stability AI, Midjourney, Adobe Firefly, Microsoft Designer, Snap AI, xAI) need to demonstrate "robust safeguards" by Dec 2, 2026. **LedgerProof receipts ARE robust safeguards** — every output anchored, auditable, provably checked against prohibited-content classifier at generation time.

New buyer persona: **Trust & Safety / Head of Responsible AI** at image-gen vendors. Net new sales pipeline.

### HRAIS extension is a sales OPPORTUNITY, not a setback
The 16-month extension is framed by regulators as "breathing room to build governance properly, not an excuse to stall." Smart GCs will use the extra time to build on the right protocol — the open standard — rather than retrofitting later.

**Pitch language for FSI:** *"You now have 16 extra months to build the right governance architecture. Don't waste it. Build on the open standard before Article 5 prohibitions and Article 50 transparency hit August 2."*

### Small mid-caps expansion = TAM grows by ~5×
Companies with ≤750 employees AND ≤€150M revenue now get simplified compliance frameworks. This is the SMB sweet spot for LedgerProof's self-serve pricing tier. Personal Exposure Calculator should target this segment explicitly.

### July adoption creates a press cycle
The Omnibus is formally adopted by Parliament + Council in July 2026 — exactly when LedgerProof's July 6 public launch occurs. **Position the launch as "the implementation layer for the AI Act, the same week the EU finalizes the Omnibus framework."** Politico Europe + FT Brussels + Handelsblatt will all bite.

### Counter-narrative monitoring
**Digital Omnibus warning** to monitor: future delays are possible but increasingly unlikely. If announced, LedgerProof's response: *"Delay creates additional time for institutions to build the implementation layer properly, not less. The protocol question doesn't depend on the enforcement date."*
