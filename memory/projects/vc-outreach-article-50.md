- **No direct quotes verified** for "AI thesis" statements at most firms. Validate via each firm's blog / LinkedIn before approaching with attributed positioning.
- **Partner names** are based on publicly listed senior GPs. For each, verify current employment via LinkedIn before sending email.
- **Cap-table data** for non-public companies (Reality Defender, Truepic) is from press releases — actual ownership percentages are not disclosed.
- **EU sovereign-fund processes** are slower than commercial VCs. Treat as Series A or post-launch follow-on candidates, not seed.
- **The competitive landscape evolves fast.** Re-run this analysis quarterly. Funds reposition; new funds launch; partners move.
