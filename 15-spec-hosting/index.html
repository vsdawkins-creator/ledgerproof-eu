spec.ledgerproofhq.io Open Cryptographic Provenance Protocol for AI System Outputs (OCPP-AI) and companion specifications — maintained by the LedgerProof Foundation (in formation)

This domain hosts the canonical, citable specifications, JSON-LD contexts, conformance materials, and Bitcoin-anchored governance event records published by the LedgerProof Foundation in support of the transparency obligations of Article 50 of Regulation (EU) 2024/1689 (the EU AI Act). All material is published under Creative Commons Attribution 4.0 International (CC BY 4.0). Reference implementations are published under Apache License 2.0.

Specifications

DocumentStatus
OCPP-AI v1.0 Core Specification
EU legislative styling (Recitals + 10 Articles + 3 Annexes). Defines the receipt structure, canonical encoding (CBOR), Ed25519 signatures, SHA-256 Merkle aggregation, and the four Article 50 sub-obligation profiles (ChatbotSession, GeneratedContent, HumanReview, manner-of-disclosure).		 Draft for stakeholder consultation
Anchor Interface Specification v1.0
Substrate-agnostic specification defining eight normative properties (I-1 through I-8) that any conforming cryptographic anchor substrate must satisfy. Bitcoin OP_RETURN as current reference implementation; European Blockchain Services Infrastructure (EBSI) as the primary enterprise-government alternative; dual-anchor deployment recommended for EU-jurisdictional deployers.		 Draft for stakeholder consultation
